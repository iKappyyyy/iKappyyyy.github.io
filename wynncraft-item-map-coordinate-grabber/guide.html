How It Works

Welcome to the Wynncraft Item Map Coordinate Grabber!

Here's how it works:

Have you ever wondered where you can obtain certain items in Wynncraft? Well, as of not so recently, a feature was added to the Wynncraft website that allows you to do exactly that!

And that feature is.... *drum rolls* The Wynncraft Item Guide™!

In short, The Wynncraft Item Guide™, is a feature that lets you get info on every single item in the game.

And one of those features just so happens to be, the ability to check where to get every item!


For example, let's take Morph-Gold, The boots part of the Morph Set.

To see where we can obtain Morph-Gold, all we need to do is search it up in the search bar on the left of the screen, hover our mouse over the requested item, followed by the location icon, where it would tell us that the morph-gold item can be obtained in Loot Chests between the levels of 21 and 29. Wow!

You can do this on mobile too, the only differences are as follows:

  • The search bar will be on top of the screen rather than on the left
  • You will have to tap the item instead of hovering over it to reveal the icons
  • You will need to hold the location icon rather than hover over it

Now, some of the items actually contain another *secret* feature, something which I like calling, The Wynncraft Item Map™

For these next parts I'm about to use an item called the Golden Avia Feather as an example.

The Golden Avia Feather is an item dropped by a rare mob called the Golden Avia that spawns all around Corkus.


For those who don't know, Golden Avia Feathers, are items that can be used in order to obtain Uth Runes via the Uth Shrine.


Uth Runes can be exchanged for Tol runes which will always sell for pretty good money because of their use of starting raids,

as can be seen in this video by Looni which explains a pretty good money making method using them.

After hovering some of the items, you will have the ability to open that item's Item Map in another tab.


The Wynncraft Item Map is just the normal Wynncraft Map, except, every spot which the item can be obtained in will be marked with either a red dot, or a red area.

In the case of the Golden Avia Feathers, the Golden Avias that drop them - just so happen to always be flying. Which makes it pretty confusing where the exact coordinates are.

Which is what led me to creating this tool that shows you the exact Coordinates, and the Radius in which the item can obtained in.


To use it, all you have to do is copy the link at the top of the page of your Item Map,

paste it on the homepage of this website, and search.

From there the website will do it's magic and give you all the information needed to go from there. Have fun, and happy hunting!
